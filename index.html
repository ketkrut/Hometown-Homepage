Top Three Attractions in Chiang Mai

Doi Suthep

Perched atop Doi Suthep mountain, this iconic temple offers panoramic views of Chiang Mai. Known for its intricate architecture and spiritual significance, it's a must-see destination for anyone visiting the city.

Doi Inthanon

Doi Inthanon is the highest mountain in the country. The national park features lush forests, stunning waterfalls, and scenic hiking trails.
